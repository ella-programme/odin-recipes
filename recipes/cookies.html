Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a timeless classic, and with this recipe you can now easily whip up a batch whenever you are craving some chewy and chocolatey deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • 220g flour
  • 1/2 pack baking powder
  • 60g brown sugar
  • 60g white sugar
  • 1 pack vanilla sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 100g melted butter
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 60g chocolate chips
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 190Â°C
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, both sugars, salt, and vanilla sugar in a bowl
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl and add butter
  4. Add the eggs and butter mixture to the rest
  5. Mix using a wooden spoon, then by hand
  6. Add honey and chocolate chips, and mix again
  7. Roll the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet
  8. Place in the oven and bake for 12 minutes
  9. Let cookies cool down for a bit, then place them on a rack
