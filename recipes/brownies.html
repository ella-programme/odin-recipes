Brownies

Description

These brownies are a perfect mix of intense dark chocolate flavor and deliciously crunchy pecan nuts. Ready to bake in only 15min, these fudgy treats will delight all chocolate lovers, and leave you wanting more!

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate (min. 65% cocoa)
  • 160g butter + extra for the baking sheet
  • 100g pecan nuts
  • 4 eggs
  • 160g brown sugar
  • 160g ground almonds

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at 170Â°C
  2. Lighlty roast the pecan nuts for 7 minutes
  3. Melt the chocolate together with the butter (cut up in dice)
  4. Beat the eggs for 1 minute
  5. Add the sugar, and mix gently using a wooden spoon
  6. Add the ground almonds, and mix thoroughly with the wooden spoon
  7. Add the molten chocolate to the mix, and beat the mixture for 1 minute
  8. Crush the roasted pecan nuts and add them to the mix
  9. Transfer the mix to a well-buttered baking sheet
  10. Bake in the oven for 18 to 20 minutes
  11. Let the brownies cool down at room temperature for 1 hour
