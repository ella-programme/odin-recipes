Blueberry Muffins

Description

The perfect pick-me-up for when you're feeling blue!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with liners
  2. Stir together milk, egg, and oil in a large bowl
  3. Add flour, baking powder, sugar, and blueberries; gently mix the batter with only a few strokes
  4. Spoon batter into the muffin tin
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes
